Patient presents for #27 facial composite.

Cotton roll isolation was used.

1 Carpule of 2% xylocaine with 1:100,000 epi given via right IAN block.

The preparation was completed and the existing composite and all caries was removed.

Preparation rinsed with consepsis.

Etching was accomplished with 35 % phosphoric acid for 30 seconds on enamel and 20 on dentin.

Conditioning accomplished with Leak LC bonding agent and light cured for 20 seconds.

The restoration was completed and the material used was C2 supreme plus and light cured for 30 seconds.

Restoration smoothed and polished with shofu disks and rubber tips.

Patient felt comfortable.
